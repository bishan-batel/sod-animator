Hello! Thank you for checking out this plugin, for more details for usage head over to the docs section,
this page is for those who want a simple quick start / about.

The core of the plugin started in 2022 when I watched this video from [t3ssel8r](https://www.youtube.com/@t3ssel8r), in where he discusses his way of *procedural animation* using differential equations.
I highly reccomended giving (at least the first half) of the video a watch to get a feel for this addon. Don't worry if you don't understand the math, I know I didn't when I watched this in high school and yet I still used this in many of my projects.
